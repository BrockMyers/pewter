Thank you for visiting our website! Throughout this fall, we will be hosting our annual Pewter City Pokemon fundraiser, raising money for sick and abandonded Pokemon. Here's how you can help:

The local Pokemon rescue league consists solely of volunteers and is in shambles. You can help by providing funds that would go toward a new facility and help with staff salaries. It's all part of our $1 million capital campaign. Last year alone, they helped save over 1,000 Pokemon from abusive owners. Consider giving and helping out their great cause.
On Feb. 27, National Pokemon Day, city leaders will be hosting a Pokemon are People Too Walk. This walk is to demonstrate that Pokemon have been mischaracterized by violent videogames and TV shows, and that they have feelings, too. No one should ever have to be kept in a Pokeball, and this walk will raise awareness. Cost for registration is $50 before the event. Walk-in registration is $500.

Welcome to Pewter City!

Pewter City, affectionately known as "A Stone Gray City" by its residents, is a rocky settlement amid the Rocky Mountains. It's the site of two main attractions: the esteemed Pewter Gym, which is led by renowned fitness instructor Brock, and the Pewter Museum of Science, which contains fascinating fossil exhibits of ancient Pokemon. We hope you enjoy our unique location!
